Who We Are

SIBRAH Computers and Technology Hub was founded with a clear mission: to provide accessible, high-quality ICT services that empower individuals and organizations to thrive in the digital age. Based in Abuja, Nigeria, we have grown to become one of the most trusted technology companies in the region.

Our services span the full ICT spectrum — from selling quality computers and accessories, to repairing and maintaining all types of devices, to developing custom software solutions for businesses, schools, and government institutions.

With a team of certified and experienced professionals, we deliver solutions that are reliable, scalable, and tailored to the unique needs of each client. Contact us at info@sibrahtech.com.ng or +234 808 110 1992.
